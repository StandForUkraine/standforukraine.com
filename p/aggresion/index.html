About the project

Our Goals

  • Supply Ukraine's military with weapons, ammunition, and gear to resist Russia's full-scale invasion.
  • Support veterans and war victims in recovery.
  • Shelter and feed displaced refugees.
  • Aid children, the elderly, and other vulnerable groups.
  • Strengthen independent journalism.
