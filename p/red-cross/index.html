Non-government, Humanitarian, Medical

Red Cross provides emergency assistance for victims of the Russo-Ukrainian war. We deliver first premedical and psychological aid, evacuate civilians, and provide humanitarian aid (clothes, food, hygiene products, medications, clean water, etc.). Over 30 000 people have already received humanitarian help in the first five days of armed conflict in 2022.
