Stand for Ukraine is an aggregator of reliable organizations that helps to fight the Russian invasion and overcome its consequences.

We do not accept payments; we only provide direct links to donation pages.

Our Goals

  • Help the Ukrainian military with weapons, ammunition, and supplies
  • Recover the veterans & victims of war
  • Shelter and feed the refugees
  • Aid children, elderly, and other vulnerable groups
  • Support independent press
About The Project

This website is made by IT engineers, managers, designers, writers, translators, journalists, and activists from all over Ukraine & beyond.
